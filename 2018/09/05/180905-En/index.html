英语学习笔记#2

自然

  • arroyo n. a dry gully/creek 干涸的河床/小河

  • brook n. a small stream 小河

  • creek n. a small stream, somewhat larger than a brook 小湾 小溪

  • rivulet n. a small stream 小溪，小河

  • fluvial a. of, or living in a stream or river 河流的 生长在河中的

  • Canyon n. a long, narrow valley between cliffs (周围有悬崖峭壁cliff的)峡谷

    比较

    valley 谷；山谷；溪谷；流域:

    gorge 峡谷

    gully < ravine < canyon

    gully 溪谷；冲沟

    ravine 沟壑；溪谷

  • gulch n. a step walled valley/narrow ravine 深谷 峡谷

    比较 gulf n. 海湾 深渊

  • gorge n. a narrow steep-walled canyon or part of a canyon 峡谷

  • ravine n. a small narrow steep-side valley that is larger than a gully and smaller than a canyon 深谷，峡谷

  • cape n. 披肩，短斗篷 a cloak；海角

    cape/cloak

  • cavern n. a big cave 大洞穴

  • grotto n. small carven 洞穴

  • crater n. a bowl-shaped cavity/a large hole in the top of a volcano 火山口, a pit made by an exploding bomb 弹坑

  • crest n. top of a hill or wave 山顶 浪尖; showy 花哨的 feather on the head of birds 羽冠；纹章

  • eddy n. little whirlpool or whirlwind 旋涡，涡流

  • whirlpool n. a place with circular currents of water in a sea 旋涡

  • ford n. a shallow place in a body of water 浅滩 水浅可涉处 v. to cross by wading 涉水

    区别

    fort 堡垒

    fork 餐叉

  • geyser n. 天然热喷泉 a spring from which columns of boiling water and steam gush 冒出 into the air at intervals 间歇

  • insular a. 岛屿的 narrow-minded, illiberal 不容言论(或行动)自由的；不开明的 心胸狭窄的

    反义词 cosmopolitan 世界性的

  • isthmus n. a narrow strip of land 地峡

  • marine a. of the sea 海的 inhabiting in the sea 海中的

    This discovery is made by the marine biologists.

  • maritime a. adjacent to the sea 沿海的 nautical/ connected with ships, sailors and sailing 航海的 海员的 船舶的

  • marsh n. a tract 地带 of low, wet, soft land/swamp 沼泽地

    区别

    march v. 行军 n. 三月

  • mire n. marsh 泥沼 a troublesome situation 困境 v. to hamper 阻碍 or hold back as if by mire 使陷入困境

    反义 extricate v. 解脱困境

  • quagmire n. soft miry land 沼泽地 predicament 困境

  • swamp n. land which is always full of water 沼泽 v. to cause to have a large amount of problems to deal with 使陷入 淹没

  • morass n. marsh 沼泽地 entanglement 困境 v. 陷入困境

  • mesa n. a land formation having steep wells and a relative flat top 高台地，平顶山，桌子山

  • oasis n. a fertile 肥沃的 palce in dessert 绿洲

  • peninsula n. a piece of land surrounded by water on three sides 半岛

  • pinnacle n. spire 尖塔 a lofty 高耸的 peak/summit 山峰 顶峰

  • precipice n. a very steep or over hanging place 悬崖

  • crag n. a steep, rugged 凹凸不平的 rock that rises above others 悬崖峭壁

  • scarp n. steep slope/escarpment 悬崖 陡坡

  • ridge n. 隆起物 脊（如屋脊，山脊等）

  • shoal n. a sandbank 沙洲 where the water is shallow 浅滩，浅水处 一群（鱼等） a. 水浅的

    反义 deep a. 深的

  • strait n. 海峡 a. narrow 狭窄的

    比较 isthmus 地峡

  • stratum n. 地层 社会阶级

    复数 strata

  • cascade n. a small, deep waterfall 小瀑布

  • estuary n. an inlet or arm of the sea 河口 三角湾

  • knoll n. a hillock/mound 小山 小圆丘

    比较 knot n. 结

  • lagoon n. shallow body of water or lake near a sea 泻湖

  • moor n. open uncultivated land 旷野地 荒野 v. to hold a ship in place 使(船)停泊

  • tundra n. rolling 起伏的 treeless plain 平原 in Seberia and arctic North America 冻原,苔原
